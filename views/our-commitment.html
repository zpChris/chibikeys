Our Commitment
Keycaps

We believe no one should ever go a day feeling hopeless.

"Where there is charity and wisdom, there is neither fear nor ignorance."

- Francis of Assisi

We promise to donate 50% of our earnings to charity: water

It is the rich soil, clean water and nourishing sunshine that make our ingredients grow, and the power of nature that makes them so effective. Yet our planet is fragile. We must be mindful of the resources we use, and minimise our impact on the earth. In our efforts to become more sustainable, we are always striving for cleaner, greener ways of doing things.

We are actively reducing our footprint

We reuse and recycle as much as we can – our current target is a 20 per cent reduction in energy, waste and water consumption by 2015 (from 2010 levels) per product produced. We are proud to say that as of 2013, more than 90 per cent of Jurlique’s production site waste is reused, recycled or energy-recovered.

We are open to new ideas

If you have any questions about Chibi Keys' commitment, or any suggestions for different charities or causes you think we should support, please contact us. We love to hear your ideas!

We're on a mission to help make the world a better place. We strive to help others in need. Read more about our commitment on our blog.
